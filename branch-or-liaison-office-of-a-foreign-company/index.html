Branch or Liaison Office of a Foreign Company in Bangalore

A body corporate incorporated outside India, including a firm or other association of individuals, can open a Liaison Office (LO) or Branch Office (BO) in India. Such entities are required to obtain permission from the Reserve Bank of India under the provisions of the Foreign Exchange Management Act (FEMA).

The Reserve Bank of India considers specific eligibility criteria while sanctioning Liaison Offices and Branch Offices of foreign entities.

Overview of Branch or Liaison Office of a Foreign Company

Track Record

  • For Branch Office — a profit-making track record during the immediately preceding five financial years in the home country.
  • For Liaison Office — a profit-making track record during the immediately preceding three financial years in the home country.

Net Worth

Net worth means the total of paid-up capital and free reserves, less intangible assets, as per the latest audited balance sheet or account statement certified by a qualified professional.

  • For Branch Office — not less than USD 100,000 or its equivalent.
  • For Liaison Office — not less than USD 50,000 or its equivalent.
