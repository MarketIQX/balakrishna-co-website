One Person Company (OPC) Registration in Bangalore

A One Person Company (OPC) combines the benefits of a sole proprietorship with the advantages of a corporate structure. It allows a single individual to operate a company as both shareholder and director.

An OPC is incorporated as a Private Limited Company. It provides limited liability protection along with a structured legal framework, making it suitable for individual entrepreneurs who want to operate in a formal business environment.

Balakrishna and Co assists clients with One Person Company (OPC) registration in Bangalore, including incorporation, documentation, and compliance support.

One Person Company (OPC)

Can a foreign national form an OPC?

No. Only an Indian citizen and resident can form a One Person Company.

Is there a restriction in setting up an OPC?

Yes. An individual can incorporate only one OPC.
